Welcome

The workshop "Polytopes-Algebra-Computation" is the second in its series and will take place in Gebze Technical University on 8-9 February 2018. The first edition was in the context of Applications of Computer Algebra - ACA 2015. The focus of the workshop is on the interaction of polyhedral geometry with algebra and algebraic geometry from a computational viewpoint.

Topics of interest

Everything in the intersection of polyhedral geometry, algebraic geometry and algorithms, including but not limited to:

  • Polytopes in algebra and algebraic geometry
  • Newton polytopes
  • Toric ideals
  • Computational Ehrhart theory
  • Algebraic aspects of polytopes
  • Algebraic applications of computational geometry
  • Applications of Polytopes (e.g., statistics, combinatorics etc.)
  • Software - Normaliz, polymake, 4ti2, barvinok, gfan, LattE, etc.

Call for perticipation

You are welcome to participate and encouraged to submit short abstracts on topics related to the topics of PAC. For the registration please fill in this form before February 5, 2018.

There is no participation fee in the workshop.
Neverthelss, we would appreciate if you register as soon as possible, since it would help with the organization.
