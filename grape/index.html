HALCYON

"Heterogeneous Algorithms for Combinatorics, Geometry, Optimization and Number Theory - HALCYON" is a TUBITAK funded project in the intersection of algorithmic mathematics and computer science.
This means that it is both 100% mathematics and 100% computer science.

The duration of the project is 24 months and starts on March 2019.

There are open positions in the project. We are actively looking for research assistants.

The GRAPE project is joint between Elias Tsigaridas, Zafeirakis Zafeirakopoulos and Ayesha Asloob Qureshi.
