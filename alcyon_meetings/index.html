Welcome

The ALCYON meeting is an informal forum to discuss topics related to polyhedral geometry, algebra, computation and their applications. It will serve as a project report for the TUBITAK funded project HALCYON, but also to expand the scope and interests of the participants. Sufficient time will be reserved for dedicated problem sessions, where all participants are welcome to join. Graduate students are especially welcome.
In view of the recently accepted project GRAPE "Gr√∂bner bases, resultants and polyhedral geometry", which is a bilateral project with France, a number of positions for grad students will open. The meeting is a good opportunity for interested students to come to contact with related topics.

Topics of interest

Everything in the intersection of polyhedral geometry, algebraic geometry and algorithms, including but not limited to:

  • Polytopes in algebra and algebraic geometry
  • Newton polytopes
  • Toric ideals
  • Computational Ehrhart theory
  • Algebraic aspects of polytopes
  • Applications of computational geometry

Call for participation

There is no participation fee for the workshop.
Neverthelss, we would appreciate if you express your interest to attend as soon as possible, since it would help with the organization.
